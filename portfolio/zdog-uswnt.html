USWNT ZDOG

See the Pen Zdog USWNT by Travis John (@travis_john) on CodePen.

  • Platform: Codepenopens in new tab
  • Date: June 2019
  • Library: ZDogopens in new tab

Zdog is a new Javascript library that renders pseudo-3D elements onto a canvas. I experimented with it's semantic class structure to combine shapes and paths to recreate a 3D rendering of the United States Women's National Football Team crest to honor them as they compete in the 2019 FIFA World Cup.
